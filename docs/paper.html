Background

This project is part of the course Environmental Data Science Analytical Workflows and Scientific Reproducibility, that aims to recreate Figure 3. from Schaefer et al.(2000) study. This time-series plot shows a 9-week moving average of stream water concentrations before and after the 1989 Hurricane Hugo disturbance, based on long-term stream water quality observations in the Luquillo Mountains, Puerto Rico.

Methods

The data was imported from the Environmental Data Initiative and arranged for the creation of the figure 3 of the article (“Effects of hurricane disturbance on stream water concentrations and fluxes in eight tropical forest watersheds of the Luquillo Experimental Forest, Puerto Rico”.) A 9-week moving average was calculated for the concentration of the following nutrients: (a) potassium, (b) nitrate-N, (c) magnesium, (d) calcium, and (e) ammonia-N. The graph was created with ggplot2, and the results were compared with the original figure.
